### App 4 — RepairCo *(coming soon)*

**Tagline.** KPI and performance engine for our newest venture — keeps the business plan honest and the P&L on track.

**Status.** Coming soon. Scope coming together.

**Audience.** Mosser only.

**The problem.** RepairCo is the newest venture — and the riskiest moment for any new business is the gap between "we have a business plan" and "we know whether we're hitting it." That gap usually lives in monthly Excel reports that surface variances after the quarter's already cooked. By the time the P&L tells you what's wrong, the chance to fix it is gone.

**What it does.** Closes the gap. Pipes RepairCo's operating data into KPI dashboards mapped to the business plan, line by line. Flags variances early — at the leading-indicator level, not the lagging-financial level — so the team can correct course before anything hits the income statement. Same data feeds board-ready reporting so the venture's performance story is always one click away.

**Use cases.**

- Monthly KPI review against business plan targets — by function, by line item
- Variance flagging at the operating-metric level, before the P&L bites
- Unit economics and margin tracking — what jobs actually earn vs. what the plan modeled
- Early-warning trend lines on cost, throughput, utilization, customer mix
- Sponsor and board reporting straight out of the same dataset
